SOMUN 2025 Committees

📋 Tap on any committee below to view detailed information, agenda, and download the background guide for comprehensive preparation materials.

IP

International Press

The International Press serves as the eyes and ears of the conference, documenting debates, conducting interviews, and providing comprehensive coverage of all committee proceedings.

UNHRC

United Nations Human Rights Council

Addressing critical human rights issues and promoting fundamental freedoms worldwide through international cooperation and dialogue.

DISEC

Disarmament and International Security Committee

Dealing with disarmament, global challenges, and threats to peace that affect the international community.

UNOOSA

United Nations Office For Outer Space Affairs

Promoting international cooperation in the peaceful use and exploration of space, and in the utilization of space science and technology.

Lok Sabha

India's Lower House of Parliament

Addressing domestic policy issues and legislative matters.

WHO

World Health Organisation

Leading global health initiatives and coordinating international health responses.

UNODC

United Nations Office for Drugs and Crime

Addressing transnational crime, drug trafficking, and international terrorism.

HCC (Stranger Things)

Hawkins Crisis Council

A fast-paced crisis committee set in the world of Stranger Things, where delegates must tackle supernatural threats, government secrecy, and community chaos in real time.

ECOSOC

Economic and Social Council

Promoting international cooperation on economic, social, and environmental issues, shaping global policies, and advancing sustainable development through dialogue among member states and stakeholders.

UNCSW

United Nations Commission on the Status of Women

Dedicated to advancing women's rights, gender equality, and empowerment through international cooperation, policy-making, and dialogue on issues affecting women globally.
